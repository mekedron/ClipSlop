# iCloud sync

When enabled, ClipSlop mirrors your **prompt library** across every Mac signed into the same iCloud account. Add a prompt on your laptop, it shows up on your desktop seconds later — same mnemonics, same shortcuts, same provider overrides.

## What syncs

- Custom prompts and folders.
- Mnemonic keys and per-prompt global shortcuts.
- Provider override per prompt.
- Display mode per prompt.

## What does *not* sync

- **Provider credentials.** Your OpenAI / Anthropic API keys live in the macOS Keychain on each Mac and are never sent through iCloud. The free **ChatGPT sign-in** OAuth tokens also stay local.
- **Transformation history.** History is in-session only — never written to disk, never synced.
- **App settings.** Theme, opacity, launch-at-login are per-Mac.

## Enabling sync

Open **Settings → iCloud Sync** and flip the toggle. ClipSlop uses CloudKit under the hood — the data lives in your private iCloud Drive container, not Anthropic's or OpenAI's servers.

If you turn sync off, your prompts on the local Mac stay; only the connection to iCloud is cut. Other Macs keep their copy.

<Callout type="note">
  iCloud sync requires that you're signed into iCloud on macOS and have iCloud Drive enabled in **System Settings → Apple Account → iCloud Drive**.
</Callout>
